About me

I am a game programmer at the beginning of my career with a dream of becoming a part of something special and mindblowing in the gaming industry. I live and breathe videogames: as a career and as a hobby.

I started my somewhat professional adventure at school in Metropolia University of Applied Sciences as an Information and Communication Technology Engineer (Bachelor's degree, about to graduate at the end of 2022).
Success in school gave me the opportunity to choose a major freely and this obviously meant Game Development, even though I was adviced otherwise ("You should rather go for Software Development as they have more job openings, yada yada yada...", no regrets).

I currently work at Air Dice Games as a game programmer, designing and building awesome online gambling games. My favourite detail about this position is the heavy emphasis on sales oriented game design (a newly found passion for me).

Manor Mutiny

Third big game project in Metropolia made by a team of nine (5 programmers, 2 graphic artists and two sound designers). An arena shooter with endless waves, doom-esc graphics and movement and a lot of gore!

Porkyfly

Second big game project in Metropolia made by a team of five (3 programmers and 2 artists). A mobile game about slingshotting a hog to fly as far as possible while gaining acorns as a currency for upgrades.

Willy's World

Our first year game project in Metropolia. A 3D platformer game made by a team of four programmer students with a lot of motivation and humour.
